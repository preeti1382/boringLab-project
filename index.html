Frequently Asked Questions

What is NFT (Non Fungible Tokens)?

An NFT stands for “Non-fungible token” and is a fancy way of saying it’s a unique, one of a kind digital item that users can buy, own, and trade. Some NFT’s main functions are to be digital art and look cool, some offer additional utility like exclusive access to websites or participation in an event, think of it like a rare piece of art that can also act as a “membership” card.

What is a playable character NFT?

Playable NFTs are digital assets that can be used inside metaverse. Like user travels to an old village in metaverse they would require decoder nft to see human transaction with nature. These have major benefits over traditional non-NFT assets. For example, actual ownership, traceable transaction history, provable scarcity, interoperability just to name a few.

What Are NFTs Used For?

Real world Art and physical collectibles are valuable because they are one of a kind, while digital files can be copied and endlessly duplicated. NFTs solve this by allowing only one owner of each digital item by providing a digital certificate of ownership stored on a shared ledger known as the blockchain. This opened endless possibilities for digital content creators. Now they can monetize without the fear of duplicity.

What does Play to Earn mean?

Play to Earn is a gaming model that allows players to monetize their play or the time invested in the movie, game, and metaverse adding more value to same. Unlike traditional models, the ownership of the assets lies with the users.

What is a DAO?

A DAO is a decentralised organisation with its own rules of functioning. Decisions are taken by voting and every member has a voice.

What do I need to mint/get an NFT?

An polygon based wallet which will store all of your owned NFT. Metamask is an easy to use crypto wallet : MetaMask - A crypto wallet & gateway to blockchain apps
